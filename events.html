halls of summer

Halls of summer is an initiative aimed to bring art closer to the lives of young people, especially those who find themselves being pushed away from it. It is a series of events that are conducted in Universities to cater to their respective communities and ecosystems around it. As of today, 4 events have taken place under the Halls of Summer initiative. The inaugural edition was conducted at BML Munjal university in Spring, 2017. 3 other events followed shortly at HR College, St. Xavier’s College and Mithibai College, Mumbai respectively.
This is an event different from the others in its very nature, as it’s not competitive and/or speculative. It’s a project to bring together like (and different) minded individuals (and groups) together, to create living art-pieces across the campus.
Our paraphernalia includes papers, colours, spray cans, lights, sounds, and everything that could possibly be used as a form of expression or embellishment.
Our home is called ‘The Hall’ which is a space dedicated to have people create without expectations, fear, or purpose. Several other collaborative events take place at satellite locations throughout the campus to realize Graffiti-work and competitions for interested individuals.

slam poetry by speakeasy

Slam poetry is a free -form poetry which means that the poems do not follow ‘’traditional’’ poetry structure. It is a type of competition where people read their poems without props, costumes, or music. Participation in group is not allowed. Each poem must be of the poet’s own construction; Each poet would get three minutes (plus a ten-second grace period) to read one poem. If the poet goes over time, points will be deducted from the total score. The poet will be judged on the Creativity of the poem, way of delivery, clarity in speech and audibility. The poets can have the choice of language from either Hindi or English.

War of Djs

The art and style of DJ’s began with the mixing of two identical records back and forth, extending the rhythmic instrumental segment. In the 80’s House music was born with the release of the very first house music album. Shortly thereafter, house music poured into the mainstream. The era of the 90’s created the rave scene with acid houses and the birth of digital music. This growing industry spawned on the birth of the DJ Superstars.
For those inspired to become a future DJ, War of DJs is a competition where DJs come and compete to show off their talent and skills. Each of the participant DJ will be given a time slot of 20 minutes for their performance including setup time and they can play any genre of their own preference. It is mainly judged on their technical abilities to mix tracks, stage confidence and crowd control. The one with maximum total of points combined from all the judging criteria wins. Prizes will be awarded to the 1st and 2nd Position worth INR 15K and INR 10K respectively. The first position will receive reward in kind from the sponsored company.

Model United Nations by Sierra

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation and/or academic activity in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. MUN involves and teaches participants speaking, debating, and writing skills, in addition to critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership abilities.
Participants of MUN are known as delegates, who represent the view point of a country on a global issue.
MUN is a unique in itself form of devate, where not only the way you talk matters but also what you talk about. It gives the participants a chance to assume roles of international leaders and delve into solving complex problems.
“This is our world, a common world, everyone should feel a common responsibility” -Ban-ki Moon, former Secretary General, United Nations
A MUN is just a step towards that realization
