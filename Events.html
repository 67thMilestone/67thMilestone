slam poetry by speakeasy

Slam poetry is a free -form poetry which means that the poems do not follow ‘’traditional’’ poetry structure. It is a type of competition where people read their poems without props, costumes, or music. Participation in group is not allowed. Each poem must be of the poet’s own construction; Each poet would get three minutes (plus a ten-second grace period) to read one poem. If the poet goes over time, points will be deducted from the total score. The poet will be judged on the Creativity of the poem, way of delivery, clarity in speech and audibility. The poets can have the choice of language from either Hindi or English.


battle of bands by pfa

Battle of the Bands is a distinctive event which involves various bands disclosing their skills in different genres related to music. This serves as an opportunity for new and undiscovered bands to showcase their talents in musical arrangement, song composition, instrumentation, and vocals.


beat the street

Beat the Street is a one -of-its-kind group dance competition that tests finesse and artistry in showcasing coordinated group choreography. With an equal weight in judgment given to execution, presentation and creativity, it is fashioned to test the unity of the participating teams.


unplugged

Unplugged is a group singing event where the teams bring out the true soul of music and cover the best songs using basic instruments and impress the crowd.
