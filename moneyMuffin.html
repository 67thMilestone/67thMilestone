Money Muffin




Money-Muffin is an initiative to help the students and clubs raise funds for their projects, tours or excursions related to academia or any activity contributing to their personal growth and exposure. Such funds have, in the past, been raised as a grant by the University or by personal means. It started off in 2017, as a means to fund the Four Horsemen for their participation in The Hult Prize Singapore, and since then there has been no looking back. We have already had 2 editions of Money Muffin, with it getting bigger and better with each successive attempt.The nature of the event is such that it is customisable as per the needs of the organisers, with only requirement being that they stick to the crux, which is organising a “food centric fest”.

The first edition , held on 13 and 14 Feb, 2018 witnessed a footfall of around 1200 , and ended up raising Rs. 75,000 for the organisers. There were about 10 stalls , with each stall making revenues above 30,000.

For the second edition held on 24-25 October, 2018, which witnessed 12 stalls, a footfall of 1500 students, food competitions and ended up raising a total amount of 1,00,000 rupees for the organisers, with each vendor making revenues of 50,000 or above.

For the third edition of Money Muffin we estimate to bring in more than 15 vendors, a footfall of more than 2500, coupled with the tunes of the ‘Local Train’. Not only is this event going to be centred around our university, but aims to target 20 or more colleges from all parts of the country.
