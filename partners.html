67th milestone partners

By partnering with 67th Milestone, you develop a strategy to promote your products and services to a large audience throughout the time until the event is done. Your participation confirms to our members your ongoing commitment to best practices, education, high-value products and technology, and improvement of the industry as a whole. Most importantly, we get you in front of our audience — helping you make a strong statement with just your presence and nurture relationships to ensure ongoing success. Since our in-house population and subscribers are diverse in nature thereby focused in possibly related products and services, your partnership of the 67th Milestone is not diluted as in other associations that have a much broader focus.

Our philosophy of collaboration has always been about ensuring satisfaction to our providers. We aim to establish 67th Milestone not only to be one of the most successful student organized fests, but also to be an influential platform for our partners to showcase themselves in the finest way possible. Partnering with us is appealing to many because of the perceived status, undoubted tangible and intangible rewards, and endorsement to your support for emerging flairs and experience in the profession. Also, as a partner, you not only influence how well your organization is showcased, but also aid us in carrying out our completely student run fest diligently.
